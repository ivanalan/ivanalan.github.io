Crop Pricing

HumaniTech

Aug 2020 - present

I am currently part of a research group at Georgia Tech called HumaniTech. I am part of a subteam that is focused on creating a web application for Kenyan farmers to view the average crop prices in the area. This is important as many farmers have a poor understanding of the prices of other farmer's crops and often undervalue their crops.

I am currently leading the UI / UX efforts. Currently, I have worked on creating user research, user stories, and prototypes for a mobile app. In the future, I plan to use react to develop the front end web application.
